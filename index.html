Welcome to Visa Status Finder

This website functions as a comprehensive visa checker that helps you quickly determine visa requirements based on your passport(s) and travel route. Simply select your passport(s) and destinations to see whether you need a visa, explore visa-free countries, eVisa and visa on arrival conditions, including how long you are permitted to stay.

  • Single Trip: Check visa requirements for a single journey by selecting your main passport, an optional secondary passport, your departure country, connection stops, and final destination. This option allows you to determine whether you need a visa for each stage of your trip.
  • Multi Trip: Use this feature to review visa requirements for multiple destinations using the same passport(s), all in one place, including visa-free, eVisa, and visa on arrival options.
  • Passport Info: View detailed visa status and visa requirements for each destination based on a specific passport, including permitted length of stay and entry conditions.
  • Main Passport: The passport you normally travel with and use to check visa requirements.
  • Secondary Passport: Use this option to compare travel access and visa requirements if another passport provides more favorable entry conditions.

This information is constantly reviewed and updated, however, it is provided for general guidance only.
